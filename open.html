How to read snapshot name:

Format: YYwWWnTTTTn
- YY = last 2 digits of the year (2025 → 25)
- w = “week”
- WW = two-digit week number (e.g. Oct 31, 2025 → week 44)
- n = letter identifier (a, b, c, etc.)
- TTTT = time in 24-hour format (e.g. 1:18 AM → 0118)

How to play Falconcraft:

  • Left-click → Mine stuff
  • W → Forward
  • A → Left
  • S → Backward
  • D → Right
  • Spacebar → Jump
